Inspirational Quotes that Stand the Test of time.

Throughout history, many influential figures have shared words of wisdom that continue to inspire us today.
Here, we explore some memorable quotes from prominent individuals, shedding light on their insights and the impact of their words.

Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein, widely known for his theory of relativity (ToR), is also remembered for his perspectives on life and curiosity. One of his most famous quotes is:

"The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existence."

Helen Keller

Helen Keller, a remarkable advocate for those with disabilities, achieved incredible accomplishments despite being both deaf and blind.

In her autobiography, The Story of My Life, she writes "Keep your face to the sunshine and you cannot see a shadow."
