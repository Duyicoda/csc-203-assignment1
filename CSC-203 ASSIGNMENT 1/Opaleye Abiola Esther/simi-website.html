VERSACE

Versace is a luxury Italian fashion house founded by Gianni Versace in 1978. Known for its bold, glamorous, and often provocative designs, it blends classic sophistication with modern opulence. Versace is recognized for its iconic Medusa logo, intricate prints, and vibrant colors. The brand offers a wide range of high-end products, including clothing, accessories, fragrances, and home furnishings.

CHANEL

Chanel is a prestigious French fashion house founded by Gabrielle "Coco" Chanel in 1910. The brand revolutionized women's fashion by introducing more casual elegance, freeing women from the constraints of corsets. Known for its iconic tweed suits, little black dress, and Chanel No. 5 fragrance, Chanel remains synonymous with timeless sophistication and luxury.

GUCCI

Gucci is an iconic Italian luxury fashion brand founded by Guccio Gucci in 1921 in Florence. Known for its high-quality craftsmanship, the brand is famous for its bold, luxurious designs, often featuring the signature green-red-green stripe and interlocking GG logo. Gucci offers a wide range of products, including clothing, accessories, footwear, and fragrances, blending traditional elegance with modern trends.
